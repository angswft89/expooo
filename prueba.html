What is The Met Gala?
The Met Gala, officially known as the Costume Institute Gala at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York, is one of the most prestigious and anticipated events in the fashion world. It is held annually with the goal of raising funds for the Costume Institute, the museum’s division dedicated to the history of clothing. Beyond its charitable purpose, the Met Gala marks the opening of the museum’s annual fashion exhibition and revolves around a specific theme. This theme sets the dress code for attendees, resulting in spectacular, avant-garde, and often iconic outfits. The Met Gala red carpet is much more than a fashion show: it’s a space where celebrities, designers, artists, and influential figures come together, creating a unique fusion of art, pop culture, history, and style.
